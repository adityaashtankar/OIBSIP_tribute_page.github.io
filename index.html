Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is a renowned Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national cricket team. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, Kohli is considered one of the finest batsmen of his generation.

ODI

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. He has achieved remarkable success in One Day Internationals (ODIs) and has numerous records to his name.Kohli holds several records in ODI cricket. As of September 2021, he has the fastest century in ODIs, reaching the milestone in just 52 balls against Australia. He also holds the record for the fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in ODI cricket. In addition, Kohli has the most centuries in successful run-chases in ODI history. Kohli has been a prolific run-scorer for India in ODIs and has played a crucial role in the team's success. He has consistently maintained a high batting average and has been a reliable anchor for the Indian batting lineup. His ability to chase down targets and perform under pressure has earned him a reputation as a reliable match-winner.

Test

Kohli holds several records in Test cricket. He is the fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in terms of innings played. He also holds the record for the most double centuries as a captain for India. Additionally, Kohli has the highest number of centuries while chasing in Test cricket. Under Kohli's captaincy, the Indian Test team achieved significant milestones, including a historic series victory in Australia in 2018-19, where India became the first Asian team to win a Test series Down Under. Kohli has been a vital cog in India's batting lineup and has been known for his aggressive approach and ability to dominate the opposition bowlers. His consistency and ability to score runs in all conditions have earned him accolades and recognition as one of the best batsmen in the world.

T20

Virat Kohli's performance in T20 cricket has also been impressive. While he has primarily made a name for himself in Test and ODI formats, he has made notable contributions in T20 internationals as well. Kohli has achieved several milestones in T20 cricket. He is the leading run-scorer for India in T20 internationals and has the most centuries in the format. He also holds the record for the fastest player to score 1,000, 2,000, and 3,000 runs in T20 internationals. Kohli's batting style in T20s is characterized by his ability to find gaps and rotate the strike, combining excellent technique with innovative shot-making. While he may not possess the power-hitting capabilities of some other T20 specialists, his ability to construct innings and play calculated shots has made him a formidable presence in the format.

Milestones reached by Virat Kohli
